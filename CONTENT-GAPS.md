# Curriculum gaps — literacy vs “fully learned”

MasteryCap = **retail foundation + risk frameworks**. Not pro mastery.
Outside always: live broker UX, your tax rules, years of journaled practice, real-money psychology.

## Ranked gaps by track (what each skips)

### 1. Stocks & Options — biggest depth hole
Skips: full Greeks book (charm/vanna), vol surface, earnings IV crush playbooks, multi-leg construction beyond verticals, assignment/exercise mechanics deep, portfolio margin, 13F/flow literacy.
Enough for: calls/puts, intrinsic/extrinsic, theta/IV awareness, covered call / vertical intro.

### 2. Crypto & Perps
Skips: chain-specific L2 risk, MEV, validator/staking economics deep, CEX vs DEX order types at *your* venue, funding arb desks, options on crypto.
Enough for: spot vs perp, funding, liquidation intuition, sizing, on-chain unlock pressure literacy.

### 3. Investing: PSX & Beyond
Skips: full financial-statement analysis, DCF, sector comps, corporate actions deep, PK tax/CGT detail, US 401k/IRA specifics.
Enough for: PSX framing, IPO skepticism, expense-ratio drag, emergency→invest layering.

### 4. Futures
Skips: calendar spreads, roll yield math deep, commodity seasonality books, exchange-specific margin schedules, options on futures.
Enough for: obligation vs option, tick/multiplier, margin waterfall, micro contracts for sizing.

### 5. Forex
Skips: central-bank playbooks, carry trade regimes, NDFs, broker A-book/B-book forensics, session algos.
Enough for: pips/lots, sessions, correlation caution, leverage-as-rope.

### 6. Spot vs Derivatives
Skips: basis trading desks, listed vs OTC legal, clearinghouse failure modes.
Enough for: ownership vs contract, when spot is the honest choice.

### 7. Bots & Copy Trading
Skips: writing strategies, exchange API security hardening, latency arb.
Enough for: grid vs DCA honesty, overfitting, signal-seller incentives, martingale ruin.

### 8. Binary Options — intentionally shallow
Skips: “how to win” (by design).
Covers: breakeven math, EV bleed, martingale trap, harm reduction. **Do not expand into playbooks.**
